Welcome to CS/ECE 4/599!

by Kyle Hale January 5, 2026

I'm excited to teach this research course on memory systems at OSU! We'll be covering a lot of ground, focusing on systems software research, but touching areas in memory technologies, fault tolerance, distributed systems, storage, hardware accelerators, and much more.

We'll use this course blog for paper discussions and project reports throughout the quarter.

This is the course blog for CS/ECE 4/599, a research-focused course on memory systems in the School of EECS at Oregon State. You can subscribe to posts on the blog with RSS.
